# Introduction to Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)

This module introduces the concept of digital twins in robotics, focusing on simulation environments that enable safe experimentation, rapid iteration, and scalable AI training for humanoid robots. You'll learn how physics-based simulation tools like Gazebo and Unity serve as the bridge between ROS 2 control systems (covered in Module 1) and AI training pipelines (covered in Module 3).

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:
1. Understand the concept of digital twins and their role in robotics development
2. Implement physics simulations using Gazebo for humanoid robots
3. Simulate various sensors (LiDAR, cameras, IMUs) with realistic noise models
4. Integrate simulation environments with ROS 2 control systems
5. Prepare simulated data for AI training pipelines
