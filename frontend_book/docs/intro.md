## Learning Approach

This textbook takes a simulation-first approach, allowing you to experiment with complex robotic systems without requiring expensive hardware. Each module builds upon the previous ones, creating a cohesive understanding of how modern humanoid robots are designed, developed, and controlled.

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Advanced undergraduate and graduate students in robotics and AI
- Researchers exploring embodied intelligence
- Engineers working with humanoid robotics
- Anyone interested in the intersection of AI and physical systems
